Chilli Miso Noodles

Description

Spicy Japanese noodle dish. Miso is a fermented paste that's made by inoculating a mixture of soybeans with a mold called koji

Ingriedients

  • Miso paste
  • Chilli Oil
  • Sesame Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Rice Wine Vinegar
  • Fresh garlic
  • Ramen Noodles

Steps

  1. Make your sauce by mixing miso paste, chili oil, sesame oil, soy sauce, rice wine vinegar, garlic and warm water. Set aside.
  2. Next, cook ramen according to the package instructions. In a big bowl, add in your cooked ramen with a spider or tongs, bringing a tiny bit of ramen water in the bowl as well while transferring.
  3. Toss over your sauce and toss until it is mixed throughout.
  4. Toss over your sauce, add in scallions and toss until it is mixed throughout.
  5. Plate and Serve.
